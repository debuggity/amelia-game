🎮 CONTROLS

Movement:
← → : Walk Left/Right
↑ : Jump
↓ : Crouch

Ground Attacks:
Q : Hadoken
W : Upper Jab
A : Forward Punch
S : Kick
Z : Flying Kick
X : Round Kick

Crouch Combos:
↓ + A : Down Punch
↓ + S : Low Kick
↓ + Z : Roll Forward

Air Attacks:
Air + A : Jump Kick
Air + → + A : Forward Kick
Air + S : Down Punch
Air + W : High Kick
